North Carolina

I was lucky enough to be born in this great state. However, I did not realize how wonderful it was until I moved around the US and spent time in other places. Now I often ponder on eventually moving back to the place I couldn't await to escape when I was younger.
The pages of this website are correlated with the cities I have spent most of my time in North Carolina. I grew up in Mount Airy and eventually lived in Fayetteville for 4 years when I was in the Army. Raleigh is the state capitol and home to many prestigious universities.
